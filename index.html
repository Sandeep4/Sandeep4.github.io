About

Engineering leader with an entrepreneurial and innovative mindset focused on solving customer problems, with 13 years of experience in Software Engineering and Product Development, including 6 years in a people management role.

I was born and raised in Nainital, where I completed most of my schooling at St. Joseph's College. After completing my higher secondary education, I earned a B.E. in Computer Science and Engineering from Kumaon Engineering College, Dwarahat. I further pursued an MS (by Research) in Artificial Intelligence from IIT Madras.

Interests and Skills

Engineering Management and Leadership, Software Design and Architecture, Distributed Systems, Site-Reliability Engineering, Data Structures and Algorithms, Mobile/Web Applications, and Artificial Intelligence.
