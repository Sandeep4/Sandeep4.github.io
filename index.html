About

<>Engineering leader with an entrepreneurial and innovative mindset focused on solving customer problems, with 13 years of experience in Software Engineering and Product Development, including 6 years in a people management role.

I was born and brought up in Nainital and did the most of my schooling from St. Joseph's College, Nainital. After 10+2, I completed my B.E. in Computer Science and Engineering at Kumaon Engineering College, Dwarahat. And then did MS (by Research) in Artificial Intelligence from IIT Madras

Throughout my career, I have developed software across various business areas. I began by building a social network at Gupshup , followed by developing a payment wallet system at Paytm. I then contributed to the creation of an AI-powered chat software company at Verloop . At Grab, I worked on developing ride-hailing and card payment gateway systems. My journey continued at Meta, where I focused on supply chain software solutions. Subsequently, I worked on Robo-taxi and remote driving technology at Motional. Currently, I am engaged in AdTech at Rokt.
