About

I was born and brought up in Nainital and did the most of my schooling from St. Joseph's College, Nainital. After 10+2, I completed my B.E. in Computer Science and Engineering at Kumaon Engineering College, Dwarahat. And then did MS (by Research) in Artificial Intelligence from IIT Madras

I started my professional career at Gupshup(Webaroo) in Mumbai, working as a backend engineer. Later I moved to Paytm in Bangalore, following which I was a founding engineer at Magictiger (now Verloop). Currently I am working as an Engineering Manager with Grab in Singapore since Jan 2017.
